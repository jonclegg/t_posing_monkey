# Privacy Policy for T-Posing Monkey

**Last Updated: December 9, 2024**

## Overview

T-Posing Monkey ("the App") is a mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Player Name/Initials
When you submit a high score or join a multiplayer game, you provide a player name or initials (up to 3 characters). This is a nickname you choose and does not need to be your real name.

### Game Scores
We collect your game scores when you submit them to the global leaderboard.

### Multiplayer Session Data
When playing multiplayer games, we temporarily store game session data including your chosen player name and in-game position. This data is automatically deleted when the game session ends.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (real name, email, phone number, address)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Payment information
- Photos or media
- Contacts
- Health data
- Browsing history

## How We Use Your Information

- Display player names and scores on the global leaderboard
- Enable multiplayer gameplay sessions
- Improve the gaming experience

## Data Storage

Game data is stored on secure cloud servers (Amazon Web Services). Multiplayer session data is temporary and automatically expires. High score data is retained indefinitely to maintain the leaderboard.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only information collected (player initials and scores) does not constitute personal information.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
